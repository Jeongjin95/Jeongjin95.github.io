I am a Ph.D. student in Biostatistics at The Ohio State University, advised by Dr. Patrick M. Schnell.

```

My research focuses on statistical methods for complex biomedical and public health data, including survival analysis, causal inference, dynamic treatment regimes, and infectious disease surveillance. I am particularly interested in developing methods for estimation and decision-making under censoring, missingness, and nonrandom observation processes.
```
